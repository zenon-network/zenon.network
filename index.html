Mission

Zenon is a cryptocurrency based on a decentralized network that aims to create next-generation tools powering the digital ecosystem of the future

Scalable

Designed for scalability and enabling massively concurrent transactional throughput while preserving decentralization

Smart

A programmable cryptocurrency that enables tokenization of value and zApps development

Secure

A dual-ledger system secured by state-of-the-art cryptography based on a decentralized p2p network with distributed topology

Zenon

Zenon works in synergy with Quasar powering Network of Momentum. ZNN is used as locked collateral to spawn Pillars, Sentinels and staking. It is also used to delegate to Pillars in order to increase their weight and increase their rank in the network

Alphanet Swap Cycles

Everyone will easily swap their funds to Alphanet by using the Syrius wallet, the command-line tool, or build tools using open source SDKs. Starting with Alphanet Launch, there will be a total of 10 Swap Cycles spanning over a period of 12 months. The Swap Decay mechanism will decrease the number of funds that can be swapped with each passing cycle until the swap window is closed.
